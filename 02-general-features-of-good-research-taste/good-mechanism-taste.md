# Good Mechanism Taste

A good mechanism explains why the result happens and what else should be true.

## What Good Taste Looks Like

- The mechanism predicts heterogeneity.
- It suggests direct or indirect tests.
- It distinguishes the paper from a pure effect estimate.
- It clarifies external validity.

## What Bad Taste Looks Like

- The mechanism is a story added after the result.
- No data could distinguish it from alternatives.
- The mechanism is not connected to theory.
- The mechanism is asserted, not tested.

## Economics Lens

Ask whether the idea improves how we understand behavior, policy, markets, institutions, welfare, or causal mechanisms.

## Finance Lens

Ask whether the idea improves how we understand prices, risks, beliefs, frictions, constraints, intermediaries, firms, or investors.

## Practice Exercise

Pick one current research idea and diagnose this dimension. Write one paragraph on what is strong, one paragraph on what is weak, and one concrete test or revision.
