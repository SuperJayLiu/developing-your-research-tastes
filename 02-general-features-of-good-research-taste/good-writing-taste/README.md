# Good Writing Taste

Good writing makes the research contribution legible without hiding complexity.

## What Good Taste Looks Like

- The introduction states the puzzle, answer, method, and contribution clearly.
- Figures teach the argument.
- The paper distinguishes facts, assumptions, and interpretation.
- The contribution is repeated consistently.

## What Bad Taste Looks Like

- The writing sounds impressive but vague.
- The paper overuses jargon.
- The contribution changes across sections.
- The reader cannot explain the paper after the introduction.

## Economics Lens

Ask whether the idea improves how we understand behavior, policy, markets, institutions, welfare, or causal mechanisms.

## Finance Lens

Ask whether the idea improves how we understand prices, risks, beliefs, frictions, constraints, intermediaries, firms, or investors.

## Practice Exercise

Pick one current research idea and diagnose this dimension. Write one paragraph on what is strong, one paragraph on what is weak, and one concrete test or revision.
