# 01 — Train Your Taste Model

This is the core of the repo.

A taste model is your internal system for judging research quality. It helps you decide:

- Is this question worth asking?
- Is this theory promising?
- Is the evidence convincing?
- Is the mechanism real or just a story?
- Is the contribution strong enough?
- What would change my mind?

This folder turns research taste into a training loop.

```text
Read → Extract Skill → Build Theory → Derive Predictions → Test → Update Taste
```
