# Taste Model Scorecard

Use this scorecard to evaluate your own research idea.

| Dimension | Question | Score |
|---|---|---|
| Question | Is the question important, not just clever? | 1–5 |
| Contribution | What would people believe differently after this paper? | 1–5 |
| Mechanism | Is the mechanism clear and testable? | 1–5 |
| Theory | Does the theory generate predictions that could be wrong? | 1–5 |
| Measurement | Do the variables capture the concepts? | 1–5 |
| Identification | Would a skeptical reader trust the comparison? | 1–5 |
| Alternative explanations | Are competing mechanisms taken seriously? | 1–5 |
| Magnitude | Is the result economically meaningful? | 1–5 |
| Writing | Can the paper be explained in one clean paragraph? | 1–5 |
| Journal fit | Which audience would care most? | 1–5 |

## Update Prompt

After scoring, write:

```text
The weakest part of this idea is...
The next test I need is...
My taste model should update by...
```
