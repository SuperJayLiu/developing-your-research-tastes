# Taste Training Exercises

## Exercise 1: One paper, one skill

Read one paper. Extract exactly one transferable skill.

## Exercise 2: Three mechanisms

For one empirical fact, write three mechanisms that could explain it. Then write one test that separates them.

## Exercise 3: Journal fit

Pick one research idea. Write why it might fit a top economics journal and why it might fit a top finance journal. Then identify what would need to change for each fit.

## Exercise 4: Bad taste diagnosis

Find a weak project idea. Diagnose whether the weakness is the question, mechanism, data, identification, contribution, or writing.

## Exercise 5: Scholar imitation

Pick one scholar page. Use one of their skills to generate a new research question in your own field.
