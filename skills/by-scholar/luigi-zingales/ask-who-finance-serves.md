# Ask who finance serves

## Source Scholar

Luigi Zingales

## Generalisable Skill

Evaluate financial systems by asking which groups benefit, who bears risks, and whether private
gains align with social value.

## Evidence Anchors

- Does Finance Benefit Society?
- Financial development work
- Political economy of finance papers

## Taste Extraction Notes

### What the papers tend to test

The tests and arguments connect finance to welfare, rents, access, and political power.

### What the abstract/introduction tends to do

The introduction broadens a technical finance topic into a social-evaluation question.

### Why this skill travels

Use this skill when a finance innovation is assumed beneficial.

### Critical boundary

The boundary is normative disagreement. Good taste separates positive evidence from welfare
judgment.

## Practice Exercise

Write one paragraph on who gains and who loses from a financial product.

## How to Use This Skill in Your Own Research

1. Restate your research idea using this skill.
2. Name the theory or mechanism it implies.
3. State one empirical or theoretical test that could reject it.
4. Identify one rival explanation.
5. Write the introduction paragraph that would make the reader care.
