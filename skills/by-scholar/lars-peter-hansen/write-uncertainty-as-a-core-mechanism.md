# Write uncertainty as a core mechanism

## Source Scholar

Lars Peter Hansen

## Generalisable Skill

Treat ambiguity, robustness, and unknown models as forces that shape decisions and prices.

## Evidence Anchors

- Robustness and uncertainty papers
- Asset pricing uncertainty work
- Macro-finance theory

## Taste Extraction Notes

### What the papers tend to test

The theory tests how agents or researchers behave when probability models are uncertain.

### What the abstract/introduction tends to do

The introduction frames uncertainty not as an error bar but as an economic object.

### Why this skill travels

Apply this in climate finance, macro risk, asset pricing, and policy.

### Critical boundary

The risk is abstraction without measurement. The paper should say how uncertainty appears in data.

## Practice Exercise

Write one observable implication of ambiguity aversion.
