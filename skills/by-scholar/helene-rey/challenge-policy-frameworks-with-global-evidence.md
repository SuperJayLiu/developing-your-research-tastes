# Challenge policy frameworks with global evidence

## Source Scholar

Hélène Rey

## Generalisable Skill

Use empirical facts about capital flows, leverage, and asset prices to test whether standard policy
frameworks are incomplete.

## Evidence Anchors

- Dilemma not Trilemma
- Global Financial Cycle papers
- International transmission studies

## Taste Extraction Notes

### What the papers tend to test

The tests examine whether global risk factors constrain national monetary policy despite exchange-
rate regimes.

### What the abstract/introduction tends to do

The introduction sets up a canonical framework and then shows the empirical pattern that makes it
uncomfortable.

### Why this skill travels

Use this skill when a standard framework ignores a powerful common factor.

### Critical boundary

The boundary is identification of the global factor versus correlated shocks.

## Practice Exercise

Pick one policy framework and identify the global variable it underweights.

## How to Use This Skill in Your Own Research

1. Restate your research idea using this skill.
2. Name the theory or mechanism it implies.
3. State one empirical or theoretical test that could reject it.
4. Identify one rival explanation.
5. Write the introduction paragraph that would make the reader care.
