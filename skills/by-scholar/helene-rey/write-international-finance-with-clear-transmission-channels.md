# Write international finance with clear transmission channels

## Source Scholar

Hélène Rey

## Generalisable Skill

Make cross-border effects credible by tracing channels from global shocks to domestic variables.

## Evidence Anchors

- International transmission papers
- Global financial cycle work
- Capital flow studies

## Taste Extraction Notes

### What the papers tend to test

The tests distinguish channels such as banks, asset prices, exchange rates, and risk premia.

### What the abstract/introduction tends to do

The introduction usually names the transmission mechanism early to avoid vague globalization claims.

### Why this skill travels

Apply this by drawing a shock-to-outcome chain with institutions on each link.

### Critical boundary

The risk is black-box global-factor regressions.

## Practice Exercise

Map one international shock into three possible domestic channels.

## How to Use This Skill in Your Own Research

1. Restate your research idea using this skill.
2. Name the theory or mechanism it implies.
3. State one empirical or theoretical test that could reject it.
4. Identify one rival explanation.
5. Write the introduction paragraph that would make the reader care.
