# Use cross-country variation with institutional caution

## Source Scholar

Raghuram Rajan

## Generalisable Skill

Exploit international variation while explicitly discussing institutional comparability and omitted
political economy.

## Evidence Anchors

- Capital structure international evidence
- The Great Reversals
- Financial development studies

## Taste Extraction Notes

### What the papers tend to test

The tests compare countries but interpret results through legal, political, and institutional
differences.

### What the abstract/introduction tends to do

The writing uses cross-country facts to generate puzzles, not as automatic causal proof.

### Why this skill travels

Apply this by pairing cross-country regressions with institutional narratives and robustness.

### Critical boundary

The risk is broad cross-country overclaiming. Good taste narrows mechanisms and alternative
explanations.

## Practice Exercise

For one cross-country fact, list three institutional confounds.
