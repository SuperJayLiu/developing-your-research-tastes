# Treat financial development as political economy

## Source Scholar

Raghuram Rajan

## Generalisable Skill

Ask who benefits from financial development or repression and how incumbents, politics, or crises
shape finance.

## Evidence Anchors

- The Great Reversals
- Financial development work
- Governance papers

## Taste Extraction Notes

### What the papers tend to test

The tests examine historical reversals, institutions, and political incentives behind financial
systems.

### What the abstract/introduction tends to do

The introduction often asks why seemingly efficient financial arrangements do not persist or emerge
everywhere.

### Why this skill travels

Use this skill in banking, capital markets, fintech, or development finance.

### Critical boundary

The boundary is that political stories need evidence, not just plausibility.

## Practice Exercise

Identify the coalition that benefits from one financial regulation.

## How to Use This Skill in Your Own Research

1. Restate your research idea using this skill.
2. Name the theory or mechanism it implies.
3. State one empirical or theoretical test that could reject it.
4. Identify one rival explanation.
5. Write the introduction paragraph that would make the reader care.
