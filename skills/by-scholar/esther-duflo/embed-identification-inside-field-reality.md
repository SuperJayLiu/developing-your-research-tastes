# Embed identification inside field reality

## Source Scholar

Esther Duflo

## Generalisable Skill

Build credible designs that respect how people, organizations, and governments actually implement
policies.

## Evidence Anchors

- School construction in Indonesia
- Field experiment papers
- Poverty intervention studies

## Taste Extraction Notes

### What the papers tend to test

The tests use field variation or randomization, but they also track compliance, dosage, operational
constraints, and behavior.

### What the abstract/introduction tends to do

The writing often explains the institutional setting early because the design is not credible
without it.

### Why this skill travels

Apply this by treating implementation details as part of research design, not as background color.

### Critical boundary

The danger is losing clean inference when implementation is messy. The page should distinguish
design validity from operational complexity.

## Practice Exercise

For one policy, list five implementation details that could change the estimated effect.

## How to Use This Skill in Your Own Research

1. Restate your research idea using this skill.
2. Name the theory or mechanism it implies.
3. State one empirical or theoretical test that could reject it.
4. Identify one rival explanation.
5. Write the introduction paragraph that would make the reader care.
