# Treat treatment effects as heterogeneous

## Source Scholar

James Heckman

## Generalisable Skill

Ask whose treatment effect is being estimated and why effects differ across people, margins, or life
stages.

## Evidence Anchors

- Treatment-effect heterogeneity work
- Human capital evaluation papers
- Program evaluation methods

## Taste Extraction Notes

### What the papers tend to test

The tests distinguish average, marginal, local, and policy-relevant treatment effects.

### What the abstract/introduction tends to do

The introduction avoids one-number thinking and ties effects to policy margins.

### Why this skill travels

Use this skill when programs affect heterogeneous populations.

### Critical boundary

The risk is heterogeneity fishing. Theory should guide which dimensions matter.

## Practice Exercise

Write three groups for whom a treatment should have different effects and why.
