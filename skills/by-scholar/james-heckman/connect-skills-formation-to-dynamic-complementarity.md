# Connect skills formation to dynamic complementarity

## Source Scholar

## Generalisable Skill

## Taste Extraction Notes

### What the papers tend to test

The tests look for timing, persistence, complementarities, and long-run outcomes.

### What the abstract/introduction tends to do

The introduction frames early interventions as changing trajectories, not only short-run outcomes.

### Why this skill travels

### Critical boundary

The boundary is long-run confounding and attrition.
