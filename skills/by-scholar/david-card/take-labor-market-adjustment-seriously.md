# Take labor-market adjustment seriously

## Source Scholar

David Card

## Generalisable Skill

Do not assume a policy or shock maps mechanically into employment or wages; study adjustment margins
and institutions.

## Evidence Anchors

- Minimum Wages and Employment
- Immigration and Inequality
- Labor-market studies

## Taste Extraction Notes

### What the papers tend to test

The tests consider wages, employment, composition, substitutability, and local market structure.

### What the abstract/introduction tends to do

The introduction frames labor markets as empirical objects where theory offers predictions but not
automatic answers.

### Why this skill travels

Use this skill in any market with frictions, adjustment costs, or institutional wage-setting.

### Critical boundary

The boundary is measurement: weak data can miss margins where adjustment occurs.

## Practice Exercise

List five adjustment margins for a policy shock before estimating the average effect.
