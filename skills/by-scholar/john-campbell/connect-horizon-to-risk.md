# Connect horizon to risk

## Source Scholar

John Campbell

## Generalisable Skill

Ask how the relevant investment horizon changes risk exposure and optimal decisions.

## Evidence Anchors

- Long-run risks and habit-related work
- Asset allocation papers
- Household finance work

## Taste Extraction Notes

### What the papers tend to test

The theory and tests examine how risks vary across horizons and investor types.

### What the abstract/introduction tends to do

The introduction frames average risk as incomplete without horizon.

### Why this skill travels

Apply this in pensions, household finance, climate risk, and corporate investment.

### Critical boundary

The risk is assuming investors share the same horizon or constraints.

## Practice Exercise

Write how a one-year and thirty-year investor see the same asset differently.
