# Turn data gaps into research questions

## Source Scholar

Gabriel Zucman

## Generalisable Skill

Treat missing, inconsistent, or strategically hidden data as evidence of the phenomenon rather than
only as a nuisance.

## Evidence Anchors

- Offshore wealth studies
- Tax evasion studies
- Distributional accounts

## Taste Extraction Notes

### What the papers tend to test

The empirical tests study where official data fail and what economic behavior produces that failure.

### What the abstract/introduction tends to do

The introduction often starts with the fact that standard data cannot see the object of interest.

### Why this skill travels

Use this skill when actors have incentives to hide behavior from official measurement.

### Critical boundary

The risk is speculative inference. A good paper triangulates multiple data sources.

## Practice Exercise

List three independent traces of a hidden activity you want to study.

## How to Use This Skill in Your Own Research

1. Restate your research idea using this skill.
2. Name the theory or mechanism it implies.
3. State one empirical or theoretical test that could reject it.
4. Identify one rival explanation.
5. Write the introduction paragraph that would make the reader care.
