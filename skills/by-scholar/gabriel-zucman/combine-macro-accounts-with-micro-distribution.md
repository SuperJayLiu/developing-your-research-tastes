# Combine macro accounts with micro distribution

## Source Scholar

Gabriel Zucman

## Generalisable Skill

Integrate national aggregates with microdata to study who owns income or wealth, not only how large
the aggregate is.

## Evidence Anchors

- Distributional National Accounts
- Global Wealth Inequality
- Top wealth studies

## Taste Extraction Notes

### What the papers tend to test

The tests reconcile totals with distributional records and check sensitivity to allocation
assumptions.

### What the abstract/introduction tends to do

The writing argues that macro facts without distribution miss the main economic question.

### Why this skill travels

Apply this by asking how an aggregate change is distributed across groups or balance sheets.

### Critical boundary

The boundary is imputation: distributional allocation rules must be transparent.

## Practice Exercise

Take one aggregate statistic and sketch how to distribute it across households or firms.
