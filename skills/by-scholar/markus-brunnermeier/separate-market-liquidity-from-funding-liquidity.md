# Separate market liquidity from funding liquidity

## Source Scholar

Markus Brunnermeier

## Generalisable Skill

Distinguish the ability to sell assets from the ability to finance positions, then study their
interaction.

## Evidence Anchors

- Market Liquidity and Funding Liquidity
- Liquidity crunch papers
- Macro-finance work

## Taste Extraction Notes

### What the papers tend to test

The tests/modeling show how funding constraints reduce market liquidity and falling liquidity
tightens funding.

### What the abstract/introduction tends to do

The introduction frames liquidity as a system of interacting constraints rather than one scalar.

### Why this skill travels

Apply this in asset pricing, banking, or corporate finance where balance sheets and market depth
interact.

### Critical boundary

The risk is loose liquidity language. Good taste defines the liquidity margin precisely.

## Practice Exercise

Take one liquidity claim and specify whether it is market liquidity, funding liquidity, or both.
