# Write macro-finance as mechanism first

## Source Scholar

Markus Brunnermeier

## Generalisable Skill

Make complex macro-finance arguments legible by foregrounding the amplification mechanism early.

## Evidence Anchors

- Market Liquidity and Funding Liquidity
- I Theory of Money
- Crisis papers

## Taste Extraction Notes

### What the papers tend to test

The writing typically introduces the core feedback loop before formal details.

### What the abstract/introduction tends to do

The abstract/introduction tells readers the mechanism, why it matters, and what the model/evidence
adds.

### Why this skill travels

Apply this by writing a one-paragraph mechanism summary before equations.

### Critical boundary

The risk is mechanism overload. The best introduction limits the number of moving parts.

## Practice Exercise

Write your macro-finance mechanism in four arrows.

## How to Use This Skill in Your Own Research

1. Restate your research idea using this skill.
2. Name the theory or mechanism it implies.
3. State one empirical or theoretical test that could reject it.
4. Identify one rival explanation.
5. Write the introduction paragraph that would make the reader care.
