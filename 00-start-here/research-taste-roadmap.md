# Research Taste Roadmap

## Stage 1: Recognition

Learn to recognize good questions, mechanisms, identification, measurement, and writing.

## Stage 2: Extraction

Turn papers and scholar examples into skills.

## Stage 3: Application

Use skills to develop your own theories and research designs.

## Stage 4: Testing

Test your theory against data, alternative mechanisms, falsification checks, and journal standards.

## Stage 5: Updating

Record what changed in your judgment. A taste model improves only if it updates.
