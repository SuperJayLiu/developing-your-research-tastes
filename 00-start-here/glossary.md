# Glossary

## Taste model

Your internal judgment system for evaluating research ideas, theories, evidence, and contributions.

## Skill

A reusable research move that can be applied beyond the original paper or scholar.

## Research move

The intellectual action a researcher takes: finding variation, isolating a mechanism, building a measure, designing a falsification test, reframing a question, or clarifying a contribution.

## Evidence

The paper, empirical example, dataset, story, or lecture that supports a skill.

## Anti-skill

A common bad habit that weakens research taste, such as overclaiming, mistaking correlation for mechanism, or adding robustness checks without learning anything.
