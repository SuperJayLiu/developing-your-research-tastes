## What This Journal Often Rewards

- decision-focused mechanism
- managerial or market-design relevance
- empirical rigor
- clear applied implications

## What Usually Feels Too Weak

- finance setting without managerial insight
- weak mechanism
- unclear audience

## Taste Questions Before Submission

1. What is the one-sentence contribution?
2. Why should this journal's audience care?
