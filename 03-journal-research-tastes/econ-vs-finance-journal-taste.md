# Economics vs Finance Journal Taste

Economics and finance share many standards: important questions, credible evidence, clear mechanisms, and strong writing.

But they often differ in what feels central.

## Economics-Flavored Taste

- policy variation,
- welfare implications,
- institutions,
- labor/development/public/macro mechanisms,
- causal identification,
- social and economic outcomes.

## Finance-Flavored Taste

- prices and returns,
- risk versus mispricing,
- beliefs and expectations,
- financial constraints,
- intermediaries,
- firms and governance,
- market design and trading institutions.

## Translation Exercise

Take one idea and rewrite it twice:

1. As an economics contribution.
2. As a finance contribution.

Then ask which version has the stronger audience.
