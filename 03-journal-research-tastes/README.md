# 03 — Research Tastes of Top Economics and Finance Journals

This section helps compare what different top journals tend to reward.

This is not a guide to gaming journals. It is a guide to understanding research taste environments.

A useful journal-taste page should ask:

- What kinds of questions does this journal reward?
- What level of identification, theory, or mechanism is expected?
- What makes a contribution feel too small?
- What kinds of papers travel across subfields?
- What would a skeptical editor or referee ask first?
